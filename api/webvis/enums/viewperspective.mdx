Represents the two different view perspectives available in the viewer.
They determine the definition of left and right in the viewer.

**Example**

* In the [INSIDE](#inside) view perspective, when speaking of the left wing mirror
  of a car, it is the mirror on the left side of the car, when looking from the
  inside of the car.
* In the [OUTSIDE](#outside) view perspective, when speaking of the left wing mirror
  of a car, it is the mirror on the left side of the car, when looking from the
  outside of the car.

This has an impact on the interpretation of the ViewDirection that is
passed for instance in [ViewerAPI.fitViewToDirection](/api/webvis/interfaces/viewerapi#fitviewtodirection) or
[ViewerAPI.fitViewToVolumeFromDirection](/api/webvis/interfaces/viewerapi#fitviewtovolumefromdirection).

#### See

ViewerSettingStrings.VIEW\_PERSPECTIVE

## Enumeration Members

### INSIDE

INSIDE: "insideView"

The view perspective where the left and right sides are defined from the
inside of the object.

### OUTSIDE

OUTSIDE: "outsideView"

The view perspective where the left and right sides are defined from the
outside of the object.
