The Basic Feature Package contains all essential APIs required to **create, structure, and interact with 3DSpaces**, making it the natural starting point for applications built with instant3Dhub. The SpaceAPI and the 3DSpaces API provide methods to interact and manage 3DSpaces.

With the Basic Feature Package, you can:

- Compose and structure 3D scenes by **organizing data** and building scene hierarchies
- **Interact with objects** in the 3DSpace through transformation, selection, and filtering
- Add context to 3D data by accessing metadata and **linking business information**
- **Control appearance** and state to support focused, intuitive user experiences

## Setup & Composition

### Dynamic Composition

Use the [InstanceGraphAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/instancegraphapi) to **structure your data**, combine multiple sources, and define visibility:

- Use pre-defined data sets as a starting point
- Build complex structures dynamically at runtime
- Add and remove nodes as your 3DSpace evolves

### Settings

Use the [SettingsAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/settingsapi) to **configure viewer and session behavior** at runtime:

- Adjust rendering, interaction, and quality settings
- Read and update settings programmatically
- Establish consistent defaults across your application

## Selection & Viewing

### Navigation

Use the [ViewerAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/viewerapi) to **control how users move through the scene** by switching between navigation modes:

- Choose between turntable, fly, and webvis navigation modes
- Adapt camera behavior to the use case

### Selection

Use the [SelectionAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/selectionapi) to **select & highlight nodes**, allowing inspection and connection with underlying data:

- Select individual nodes or groups of elements
- Use selection as a basis for interaction and workflows

### Show / Hide

Use the [InstanceGraphAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/instancegraphapi) to **control the visibility of individual nodes**:

- Show or hide nodes on demand
- Reduce visual complexity during inspection
- Drive visibility from selection, filtering, or application logic

### Transform

Use the [InstanceGraphAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/instancegraphapi) to **control how nodes are positioned**, oriented, and scaled within the scene:

- Visualize step-by-step working instructions
- Dynamically transform your 3D data using APIs or built-in gizmos

### Appearance

Use the [InstanceGraphAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/instancegraphapi) and [MaterialAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/materialapi) to **adjust how your 3D data is presented**:

- **Appearance & Transparency**: Highlight or de-emphasize parts by changing color and transparency
- **Render Modes**: Switch rendering styles to reveal structure or relationships
- **Shading**: Adapt lighting and shading to support different viewing situations
- **Material**: Assign and adjust materials for realistic or schematic presentation
- **Outline & Ghosting**: Emphasize relevant parts while keeping surrounding context visible

### Highlighting

Use the [InstanceGraphAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/instancegraphapi) to **visually emphasize nodes** and draw attention to relevant parts:

- Highlight nodes during inspection or guided workflows
- Combine highlighting with selection and metadata-driven logic
- Guide users to the parts that matter

### Magnifier

Use the [ViewerMagnifierAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/viewermagnifierapi) to **provide a magnified view** of a region of interest within the viewport:

- Inspect fine details without changing the camera
- Support precise interaction in dense scenes

### Animations

**Experimental**

Use the [AnimationAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/animationapi) to **animate nodes over time** and illustrate motion, sequences, or assembly steps:

- Drive node transformations through animations
- Communicate processes and dynamic behavior

### Explosions

**Experimental**

Use the [ExplosionAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/explosionapi) to **reveal internal structures** by separating geometry based on spatial relationships:

- Use explosion states to explain structure or assembly order
- Support step-by-step understanding of complex products

## Data & Inspection

### Properties & Meta Data

Use the [InstanceGraphAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/instancegraphapi) to **add context** to your 3DSpace by accessing and using existing properties and metadata:

- Read and interpret metadata already present in your models
- Link business data and identifiers to 3D structures
- Use metadata to drive filtering, logic, and interaction

### Search & Filter

Use the [QueryAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/queryapi) and the [LayerFilterAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/layerfilterapi) to **find and isolate relevant content** within your 3DSpace:

- Query nodes by properties, metadata, or structure
- Filter enabled nodes using orthogonal layer information
- Use existing layer definitions from the source data
- Isolate relevant parts of a model and reduce visual complexity

### PMI & Modelviews

Use the [InstanceGraphAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/instancegraphapi) and [VariantsAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/variantsapi) to **display Product Manufacturing Information (PMI)** and switch between **model views and variants**:

- Display PMI such as tolerances, annotations, and dimensions
- Switch between predefined model views and variants
- Communicate engineering intent directly in the 3DSpace

### Topology

Use the [TopologyAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/topologyapi) to **access B-Rep topology** such as faces, edges, and points:

- Inspect the topological structure of your geometry
- Provide the foundation for precise selection, measurement, and clipping

### Single Measurement

Use the [MeasurementAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/measurementapi) to perform **single, precise measurements** on faces, edges, and points directly in the 3DSpace:

- Measure distances, dimensions, and angles on geometry
- Support quick inspection without leaving the viewer

### Color Comparison

Use the [InstanceGraphAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/instancegraphapi) to **highlight differences between states or versions by color** for quick visual comparison:

- Compare versions or configurations at a glance
- Use color coding to surface changes

## 3DSpaces

### Live Collaboration

Use the SpaceAPI to **share a 3DSpace across users and devices** and enable synchronized, collaborative review:

- Share a 3DSpace with multiple participants
- Keep views and state synchronized across sessions
- Collaborate in real time across devices

### Snapshots

Use the [SessionStorageAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/sessionstorageapi) to **capture and restore states** of your 3DSpace to support workflows and reuse:

- Revisit specific viewpoints or states at any time
- Compare or cycle through different configurations as part of a process

### Annotation

Use the [AnnotationAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/annotationapi) to **add contextual information** directly to your 3DSpace to **support collaboration** and communication:

- Attach notes, images, or rich HTML content to 3D elements
- Provide additional context without modifying the underlying data
- Link 3D data to external systems such as ticketing or documentation tools

## Utility

### Attachments

Use the [AttachmentAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/attachmentapi) to **associate additional data with nodes** in your 3DSpace:

- Attach auxiliary data to nodes
- Retrieve attachments to drive application logic

### Collections

Use the [CollectionAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/collectionapi) to **group nodes into collections** for efficient batch operations:

- Define collections based on queries or explicit selections
- Apply operations to many nodes at once

### Events

Use the [EventAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/eventapi) to **react to changes in the 3DSpace** and build interactive applications:

- Subscribe to events such as selection, visibility, or structure changes
- Keep your application state in sync with the 3DSpace

### Coordinate Systems

Use the [CoordinateSystemAPI](/v3.14/api/webvis/interfaces/coordinatesystemapi) to **work with coordinate systems** within your 3DSpace:

- Convert between local and world coordinates
- Align data from different sources consistently
