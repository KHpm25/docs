Build **advanced engineering workflows** on top of instant3Dhub's 3DSpaces. The Advanced Feature Package covers APIs for **markups and digital mock-up** operations — from annotating and drawing in 3D to inspecting detailed topological structures, making it the next step beyond basic visualization.

With the Advanced Feature Package, you can:

- Communicate intent with **markups, drawings, and polylines**
- Guide users to relevant locations using **points of interest**
- Perform **precise, multi-entity measurements** to support engineering decisions
- Inspect inner details using **clipping planes, clipping rooms, and capping**

<Note>
Features marked with Experimental are not yet generally available (GA) and may still change.
</Note>

## Markups

### Drawing

Use the [DrawingAPI](/api/webvis/interfaces/drawingapi) and [DrawingPlaneAPI](/api/webvis/interfaces/drawingplaneapi) to **add freehand markups and sketches** onto drawing planes in the 3DSpace:

- Sketch directly on configurable drawing planes
- Communicate review notes and ideas visually
- Combine drawings with annotations for richer context

### Polylines

Use the [ViewerPolylineAPI](/api/webvis/interfaces/viewerpolylineapi) to **draw polylines** as markups or measurement aids along geometry:

- Trace paths and contours on the model
- Use polylines as visual guides or measurement references

### Points of Interest

Use the [PointOfInterestAPI](/api/webvis/interfaces/pointofinterestapi) interface to **define and navigate to marked viewpoints and hotspots** in the model:

- Mark relevant locations within the 3DSpace
- Guide users along a sequence of points of interest
- Support guided reviews and inspection workflows

## Digital Mock-Up

### Advanced Measurements

Use the [MeasurementAPI](/api/webvis/interfaces/measurementapi) to perform **complex measurements across multiple entities** and **analyze and validate geometric properties and relationships** within your 3DSpace:

- Evaluate dimensions, distances, and angles across multiple entities
- Analyze relationships between faces, edges, and points
- Support engineering workflows such as inspection and validation

### Clipping

Use the [ClipPlaneAPI](/api/webvis/interfaces/clipplaneapi) to **inspect inner details** of complex 3D data by **selectively cutting through geometry** using clipping planes and volumes:

- Define clipping planes by position and orientation directly in the 3DSpace
- Derive clipping planes from geometry features such as surfaces, normals, or edges
- Combine multiple planes into clipping rooms to isolate volumes of interest
- Interactively generate clipping volumes from nodes within the InstanceGraph

### Capping

**Experimental**

Use the [ClipPlaneAPI](/api/webvis/interfaces/clipplaneapi) to **maintain visual continuity and clarity** when inspecting clipped geometry by **closing open surfaces** created by clipping planes:

- Automatically generate capping geometry from clipped surfaces
- Associate capping surfaces directly with the originating assembly nodes
- Preserve spatial orientation and context while inspecting internal structures

Capping geometries are derived from clipping planes and remain tightly coupled to the corresponding nodes in the assembly structure.
