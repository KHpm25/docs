## What is instant3Dhub?

instant3Dhub is a **visual-computing-as-a-service** platform that streams massive CAD assemblies directly from your PLM or PDM system to any browser or device — no file downloads, no format conversions, and no client-side installs required.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your 3D data">
    Point instant3Dhub's Data Gateway at your existing PLM, PDM, or file storage via standard HTTP. Your data stays in place — instant3Dhub reads it on demand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stream and visualize">
    Users open a browser and instantly see pixel-perfect renders of even terabyte-scale assemblies, with progressive streaming loading only what's visible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collaborate in 3DSpaces">
    Create persistent 3DSpaces where teams can share sessions, add annotations, take measurements, and apply clip planes together in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build custom applications">
    Use the webvisAPI to embed 3D visualization into your own web, desktop, or Unreal Engine applications with just a few lines of code.
  </Step>
</Steps>
