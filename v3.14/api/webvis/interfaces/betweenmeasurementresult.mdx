The result of a distance and angle measurement between two [MeasurementTarget](/v3.14/api/webvis/types/measurementtarget)s.

#### See

* MeasurementAPI.measureBetween
* [MeasurementType.MULTIPLE](/v3.14/api/webvis/enums/measurementtype#multiple)

## Properties

### `Optional`angle

angle?: number

The angle between the two measurement targets if applicable.

### distance

distance: number

The distance between the two measurement targets.

* If the two measurement targets intersect the distance is zero.

### `Optional`intersection

intersection?: [MeasurementTarget](/v3.14/api/webvis/types/measurementtarget)[]

The intersection of the two measurement targets, if existent. Note
that the intersection is not available for all [MeasurementTargetClass](/v3.14/api/webvis/enums/measurementtargetclass)
combinations. For details, see the documentation of
measureBetween.

### points

points: [[number, number, number], [number, number, number]]

The two 3D points used to calculate the distance.

* If the two measurement targets intersect the two points are equal.
* If the two measurement targets intersect and both are from the class [MeasurementTargetClass.PLANE](/v3.14/api/webvis/enums/measurementtargetclass#plane) the two points are used to describe the intersection line.
