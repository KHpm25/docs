Snapshots serve as **persisted configuration states** of a 3DSpace, capturing the current state of authored space content and visibility settings at a **specific point in time**.

## Key Characteristics

- Snapshots can be **restored later** to return the 3DSpace to a previous state
- Deleting authored content from a 3DSpace also removes it from all snapshots
- Restoring a Snapshot does not recreate deleted content
